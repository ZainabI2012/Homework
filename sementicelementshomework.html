Facts about dogs


Remarkable sense of smell: Dogs have up to 300 million olfactory receptors. Color vision: They can see colors like blue and yellow. Communication: Dogs use body language, vocalizations, and scent to communicate. Domestication history: They have been domesticated for thousands of years, evolving from wolves. Breed diversity: There is a wide range of breeds, each with unique roles and physical features.
